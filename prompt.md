# Prompt: Create a Svelte Portfolio Website

You are an expert Svelte/SvelteKit developer and UI/UX designer.  
Your task is to **design and implement a modern, responsive portfolio website** for a **Business Informatics Master’s student** with strong interests in **Full-Stack Development** and **Artificial Intelligence**.

The website should:
- Be built with **Svelte** (preferably SvelteKit).
- Have a **clean, modern, professional UI**.
- Include **subtle, meaningful animations** and **micro-interactions** that enhance UX (no unnecessary distractions).
- Be fully **responsive** (desktop, tablet, mobile).

---

## 1. Brand & Personality

**Target impression:**  
The site should feel:
- Professional, yet approachable.
- Tech-savvy and modern.
- Curious, innovative, and growth-oriented.

**Style guidelines (can be refined):**
- Color palette: 1–2 primary colors (e.g. blue or teal) + neutral grays + an accent for hover states.
- Typography: clean sans-serif fonts; clear hierarchy for headings, subheadings, and body text.
- Layout: spacious, good use of white space, visually balanced sections.

---

- I’m interested in **Full-Stack**, **AI**, and **projects that combine business and tech**.
